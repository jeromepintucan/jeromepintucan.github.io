About Us

Solutions With Data is a startup born from a simple idea: that every business, no matter how small, deserves to understand and benefit from their data. We started with a mission to make data less intimidating and more empowering — to show business owners how their numbers can guide decisions, uncover opportunities, and spark growth. We're not just here to build dashboards or manage databases — we're here to bridge the gap between everyday operations and data-driven clarity. Through each project, we aim to educate, collaborate, and prove that smart decisions always start with data.

Recent Projects

  • Insurance Transaction

    The Insurance Transaction Dashboard streamlines complex policy data into an intuitive interface. It empowers decision-makers to monitor key performance indicators such as placed business, conversion rates, and claim statistics.

  • Blinkit

    The Blinkit Dashboard is a dynamic and intuitive analytics interface designed to provide real-time insights into business performance. With its clean layout and powerful visualizations, it enables users to monitor key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

  • Performance Analysis

    The Financial Overview Dashboard simplifies complex sales data into a clear, actionable interface. It enables business leaders to track essential metrics such as total sales, quantity sold, profit margins, and average sales performance.
